About Mediconnect Limited

Mediconnect Limited is a leading connector of diagnostic information in Nigeria. Our mission is to empower patients to make informed decisions about their healthcare by providing them with access to accurate and up-to-date information about diagnostic centers, location and cost.

We help patients book appointments, compare prices, and pay for diagnostic tests to ensure reduced time waiting for tests.
